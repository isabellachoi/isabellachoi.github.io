About Me

As an artist, I strive to translate private experiences into public encounters that invite viewers to look, feel, and ponder over similar moments from their lives. My artistic process focuses on employing the full expressive potential of visual art to capture emotional nuances and experiences elusive to words. As such, I am drawn to the challenge of making intangible moments utterly tangible through visual art. By introducing an experimental focus to the creative process, I seek to transcend boundaries through the universal understanding of colors and textures.
